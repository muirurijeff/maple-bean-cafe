Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Maple & Bean.

We are open Monday to Friday from 6:30am to 9:00pm, Saturday from 7:00am to 10:00pm, and Sunday from 8:00am to 6:00pm. We are open on all public holidays except Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

Walk-ins are always welcome for regular seating. For groups of 6 or more, or for private room bookings, we recommend calling us on +254 700 123 456 or using the contact form on our website at least 48 hours in advance.

We are located at Westlands Square, Ring Road, Nairobi. We are on the ground floor next to the main entrance. There is free parking available in the building basement for up to 2 hours. We are also a 5-minute walk from the Westlands Bus Stage.
