About Us

Our story, our team, our passion for great coffee.

Our Story

Maple & Bean was founded in 2018 by two childhood friends, Amina Wanjiru and David Kamau, who shared a dream of creating a warm, welcoming space in the heart of Nairobi where people could slow down, connect, and enjoy truly great coffee.

What started as a small 10-seat café in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most loved coffee spots, serving over 300 customers daily. We work directly with smallholder coffee farmers in Nyeri, Kirinyaga, and Murang'a counties — paying fair prices and building long-term relationships.

Every cup we serve carries the story of those farmers, and we're proud to bring that story to your table.
